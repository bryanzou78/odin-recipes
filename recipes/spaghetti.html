Spaghetti

Description

A basic spaghetti template that you can customize by choosing your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • Marinara/Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Choice of Spaghetti/Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Onions
  • Cheese

optional

  • Different Sauce or Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Ketchup
  • Bell Peppers
  • Chili Flakes
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 9-11 minutes
  2. Cook ground beef, onions, and garlic until medium rare and browned
  3. Simmer sauce with spices
  4. Combine and add/melt cheese onto spaghetti
