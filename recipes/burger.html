Burger

Description

A basic burger template that you can customize by choosing your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Ground Beef Patties
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mayo
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

optional

  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Mustard
  • Bacon
  • Pickles
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Toast burger buns for a few minutes
  2. Season ground beef patties and cook until medium rare, flip halfway through to cook evenly
  3. Spread Mayo and other condiments onto buns
  4. Melt cheese onto buns if desired
  5. Research the different ways to layer a burger for an hour or two
  6. Assemble the burger ingredients your favorite way
  7. Devour the burger
