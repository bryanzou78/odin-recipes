Curry

Description

Chicken curry over rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts/Thighs
  • Curry Simmer Sauce, Roux, or Powder
  • White/Brown Rice
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Paprika
  • Water

optional

  • Different Meat
  • Different Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Chili Flakes
  • Sugar
  • Coconut Milk
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breasts/thighs into cubes
  2. Cook chicken until slightly browned on all sides
  3. Cook vegetables and garlic for a few minutes
  4. Add simmer sauce, roux, or powder with water and bring to boil
  5. Add spices and coconut milk
  6. Simmer for 5-10 minutes
  7. Serve over rice
